We, as a “SAVAVY” team, organized a great project on 15th of November namely “Hour of Code.” This particular event was held in Zangilan (Karabakh) secondary school no. 29. During the event, our team members made various presentations about different topics, such as basics of programming, ADA University and etc. The project mainly focused on creating inspiration on students that we consider as a future of our society and potential professional programmers. Our brilliant Team members provided detailed information about importance of coding, learning process of it, initial steps that must be taken in order to become a programmer in the future and educational advantages of ADA University that gives students extra opportunities. In this project 25 students and 2 teachers were attending and it continued for 50 minutes. As a result, we humbly believe that our main goals of creating inspiration on students to learn programming and also in the future applying to ADA University for its great advantages in the education field were achieved.
